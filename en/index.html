Who am I?

My name is Bradley Hu, and I am a current grade 6 student diagnosed with ADHD and ASD. Before I was diagnosed, I was having a hard time at school. Schoolwork was overwhelming, and I was always teased here and there. It was only a year after I moved to Canada that I learned about my diagnosis. I feel like that if I can get the information and support, everyone should.

What is this, exactly?

ADHD/ASD is NOT a disease or a "bad" condition. Instead, it's a label — a thing that sets you apart. It also means you might be more easily distracted, angered, and so on. But it also comes with unique strengths and perspectives that the world needs.

So what are we doing?

Our mission and vision is to help people around the world understand what neurodiversity is, and to help people with those conditions reach their full potential!
