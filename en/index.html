Who am I?

My name is Bradley Hu, and I am a current grade 6 student diagnosed with ADHD and ASD. Before I was diagnosed, I was having a hard time at school. Schoolwork was overwhelming, and I was always teased here and there. It was only a year after I moved to Canada that I learned about my diagnosis. I feel like that if I get the information and support, everyone should.


ADHD/ASD is NOT a disease/"bad" condition. Instead, it's a label, a thing that sets you aside. However, it also means that you might be more easily distracted, angered, etc.
